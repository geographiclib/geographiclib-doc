Geodesic Bibliography

Errata for Jacobi's Lectures on Dynamics, translated by K. Balagangadharan (Hindustan Book Agency, 2009).

Errors

  • Title page: replace “Brockardt” by “Borchardt”.
  • p. ix, paragraph 1, line 6: replace “aequtionen differentiales partiellen primi ordinus” by “aequationes differentiales partiales primi ordinis”.
  • p. ix, paragraph 2, line 2: replace “nad” by “and”.
  • p. ix, paragraph 2, line 3: replace “Borckhardt” by “Borchardt”.
  • p. ix, paragraph 2, line 5: replace “Brochardt's” by “Borchardt's”.
  • p. 123, second equation: replace “\(ddx\)” by “\(dx\)”
  • p. 124, last equation: remove the commas from the summand.
  • p. 159, two-thirds of the way down: replace “\(p_i^0\)” by “\(p_i^\circ\)”.
  • p. 221, equation (S1), second subequation: the second term in the sum on the left hand side should include a factor “\(x_2^2\)”.
  • p. 222, equation (S2): remove braces at the ends of the first two subequations; remove ellipses at the end of the right hand side of the first subequation; in the last subsequation replace “\((a_1+\lambda_3)\)” by “\((a_1+\lambda_n)\)” and “\((a_{n-2}+\lambda_3)\)” by “\((a_{n-2}+\lambda_n)\)”.
  • p. 222, equation (26.2), second subequation: replace “\((a_2+\lambda_{m-1})(a_2+\lambda_m)\)” by “\((a_2+\lambda_{n-1})(a_2+\lambda_n)\)”.
  • p. 223, footnote: replace “Berelini” by “Berolini”; replace “p.3 f.f” by “p. 3ff.”.
  • p. 224, numerator of displayed equation following equation (26.5): replace “\((a_m+\lambda_m)\)” by “\((a_m+\lambda_n)\).”.
  • p. 226, 3rd last line: replace “(24.4)” by “(26.4)”.
  • p. 232, last line of equation (27.2): insert “\(+\)” at the beginning.
  • p. 232, equation (27.3), first term: in the denominator replace “\((c_1+\lambda_3)(c_2+\lambda_3)(c_3+\lambda_3)\)&rdquo by “\((a_1+\lambda_3)(a_2+\lambda_3)(a_3+\lambda_3)\)”.
  • p. 233, footnote: replace “Exercises” by “Exercices”; replace “fonictiones” by “fonctiones”.
  • p. 235, first paragraph, line 3: replace “(p.194)” by “(p. 192)”.
  • p. 239, line 2: replace “Lagarange's” by “Lagrange's”.
  • p. 239, two-thirds of the way down: replace “\(\mu,\nu\)” by “\(u,v\)”; after the following equation, replace “hold” by “holds” and “similarity-ratio” by “similarity ratio”

Formatting problems

  • Separate differential quantities from surrounding terms by thin spaces, e.g., on p. 239, “\(m\,ds^2\)”, “\(A\,dp^2\)”, and “\(2B\,dp\,dq\)”.
  • Consistently use the style of square root with an overbar; e.g., on p. 239, replace “\(\surd m\)” by “\(\sqrt m\)”; similarly, on p. 240, replace “\(\surd -1\)” by “\(\sqrt{-1}\)”.
  • p. 232, last paragraph, 3rd line: replace “constant\(\lambda_1\)” by “constant \(\lambda_1\)”.
  • Currently “\(\phi\)” is used in, e.g., §7, while “\(\varphi\)” is used in, e.g., §19; consistently use just one style.
  • p. 56, before equation (7.4), replace “m” by “\(m\)”.
  • p. 15, half-way down: insert a space after “although”.
  • p. 62, first paragraph: replace “Mecanique Analytique” by “Mécanique analytique”.
  • p. 121, equations in the middle of the page: the corresponding terms should be aligned by inserting extra space after the equal signs of the second and following equation.
  • p. 125, first set of equations: the layout makes a nonsense of the terms, in particular, line breaks (if they are needed) should be at commas not plus signs; if possible, revert to the original layout and alignment.
  • p. 125, equation (14.8) and the preceding equation: a term has been omitted in summand of each equation.
  • p. 152: insert spaces in “p.147” and after commas in three other places.
