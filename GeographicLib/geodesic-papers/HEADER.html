Scans of old papers on geodesics

This directory contains scans which fill in some of the gaps in the Google Books and other online collections. It also includes scans of those pages in Google Books which are missing or unreadable. The description fields within these pdf files provide full citations and, where applicable, give links to the corresponding Google Books. The bibliography for geodesics on an ellipsoid contains links to these and many other papers on geodesics.
