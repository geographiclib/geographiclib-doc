Scans of old papers on geodesics

A list of the older mathematical treatments of the geodesic problem for an ellipsoid is given here.

This directory contains scans which fill in some of the gaps in the Google Books and other online collections. It also includes scans of those pages in Google Books which are missing or unreadable. The description fields within these pdf files provide full citations and, where applicable, give links to the corresponding Google Books.

In the course of researching geodesics, I had the need for an English translation of F. W. Bessel, Über die Berechnung der geographischen Längen und Breiten aus geodätischen Vermessungen, Astron. Nachr. 4 (86), 241–254 (1826). This is provided at arXiv:0908.1824.
